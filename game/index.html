How to Play

Use left/right arrows to move your character

Press space to jump and dodge attacks

Press A to shoot bullets at enemies

Press S for special attack (requires energy)

Defeat enemies to gain score and Pepecoin

Every 1000 points, face a powerful Boss

Collect Pepecoin to boost your score

Levels increase difficulty, power-ups appear randomly
